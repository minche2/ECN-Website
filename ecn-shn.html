About Us

The Early Career Network (ECN) is a Cisco-wide Employee Resource Organization (ERO)

The Early Career Network (ECN)
is a Cisco-wide Employee Resource Organization (ERO)

Bring employees early in career together to contribute to Cisco’s success and growth through professional development and networking activities

Current State

  • 33 Chapters across 30 countries
  • Chapter maturity model defined
  • Wide range of aspiring to experienced Chapters

Short-term Goals

  • Financially independent chapters
  • Robust data on ECN community
  • Track and slice metrics based on location, organization, etc
  • Increase & capture engagement via Impact Month (March 2018)

Who can join

  • ECN is for people who are not only new to a career,
    but also for those new to Cisco. It is open to all job functions

Our Events

ECN SHN Kickoff Event

Early Career Network (ECN) event for the Shanghai Chapter at Cisco Research Development Center (CRDC) on Aug 30, 2012.
There were 10 ASR and 3 vGAM along with 14 CDRD new hires participated in the event, and we had Gene Quon, VP, GM of CRDC and Simon Kwong, Director of Engineering,
came to meet with us and held a lively and engaging discussion.

ECN Showcase week

ECN globally come together to chat with our CEO, Chuck Robbins
Chuck’s challenge to us: be the change you want to see at Cisco! 

River of Hearts(ROH) sorting event

River of Hearts (ROH) is a charity program in Shanghai. It collects the donations, mainly second hand clothing and items,  from people living and working in Shanghai.

ECN SHN members joined ROH event. It should be a real meaningful activity, all the members feel it is a lesson to teach us to feed back to our community, to be a decent and thankful person should be always in our mind.

“One Day Life in Cisco” ECN Video Contest

To promote the each ECN member comprehend the company deeper, we held a video contest with a topic “One Day Life in Cisco”.

We received five great videos from CABU, SCO, ENG, CE, and ASR during two weeks. Through global votes, the winner goes to CABU team(Qi Chen, Hansong Zhao, Perry Li).

All the video is a impressive and creative introduction of BU, the videos got a great feedback from the audiences and we are very pleased share them with all ECN members.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here you can find some common questions.

  • What the ECN is?

    ECN represents Early Career Network, is a Cisco-wide Employee Resource Organization (ERO) that brings employees early in career together to contribute to Cisco’s success and growth through professional development and networking activities

  • Where can I get the ECN activities or some latest news?

    You can find the latest Shanghai ECN news on our website. Or You can subscribe to our official mailer(ecn-shn@cisco.com).

  • How ECN can benifit me?

    ECN will invite some technical people or department leaders to train our members in terms of technology or communication skills, etc. Besides, team building is also an important part, you can know employees from different departments.
